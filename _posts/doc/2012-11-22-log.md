To use logger in goku, just:

    goku.Logger().Logln("i", "am", "log")
    goku.Logger().Errorln("oh", "no!", "Server Down!")

this will log like this:

    2012/07/14 20:07:46 i am log
    2012/07/14 20:07:46 [ERROR] oh no! Server Down!
